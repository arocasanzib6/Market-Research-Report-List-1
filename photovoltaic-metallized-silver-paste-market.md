<p><ul><li>Front Side Silver Paste</li><li>Back Side Silver Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic metallized silver paste market is divided into two primary types: front side silver paste and back side silver paste. Front side silver paste is applied to the top layer of solar cells, enhancing light absorption and electrical conductivity. It typically features high silver content for effective energy conversion. Back side silver paste, applied to the rear side, serves to improve cell efficiency, providing a conductive path for current flow while also protecting the cell's backside from environmental factors.</p></p>
