<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needlefelt</li><li>Wet laid</li><li>Melt blown</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air filtration media encompasses various types that serve distinct applications. Spunbond media features a lightweight structure ideal for dust and particulate filtration. Needlefelt is characterized by its dense fibers, offering enhanced filtration efficiency and durability. Wet laid media employs a water-based process, resulting in a uniform and fine texture, suitable for high-performance filters. Melt blown media is produced by melting thermoplastic fibers, creating a fine, porous structure that provides excellent filtration for fine particles and aerosols. Each type serves specific industrial and environmental needs.</p></p>
