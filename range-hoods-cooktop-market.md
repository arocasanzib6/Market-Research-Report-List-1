<p><p>The range hoods cooktop market is categorized into three main types: wall mount hoods, island mount hoods, and under cabinet hoods. Wall mount hoods are fixed to the wall above the cooktop, providing efficient ventilation in kitchens with a dedicated space. Island mount hoods are designed for kitchen islands, suspending from the ceiling to ensure unobstructed airflow. Under cabinet hoods are installed beneath cabinets, blending seamlessly into kitchen designs while offering practical ventilation options. Each type caters to different kitchen layouts and stylistic preferences.</p></p>
