<p><p>The PET scan devices market is primarily divided into two main types: PET scanners and PET-CT scanners. PET scanners focus solely on positron emission tomography, providing detailed metabolic imaging and assessing cellular activity. On the other hand, PET-CT scanners combine PET and computed tomography technologies, offering enhanced anatomical and metabolic information in a single scan. This integration improves diagnostic accuracy, aiding in the detection and monitoring of various diseases, particularly cancers, and optimizing treatment planning.</p></p>
