<p><p>The semiconductor grade fused quartz market is categorized into types such as Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD), Vapor-Axis Deposition (VAD), and others. CVD involves depositing layers of quartz through chemical reactions, ensuring high purity and uniformity, suited for advanced applications. VAD is a method where silane gas is converted to solid quartz, offering excellent thermal and optical properties. Other methods may include manufactured fused quartz products tailored for specific industrial needs. Each type caters to distinct requirements within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
