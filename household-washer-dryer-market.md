<p><p>The household washer dryer market includes two primary types: condenser dryers and heat pump dryers. Condenser dryers work by extracting moisture from clothes using hot air, which is then cooled to remove water, collecting it in a tank. Heat pump dryers are more energy-efficient, using a closed-loop system that recycles hot air for drying, extracting moisture without excessive heat. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with heat pump dryers being favored for their energy savings and eco-friendly benefits.</p></p>
