<p><strong>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems market includes two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF). CVAA utilizes cold vapor technology to detect mercury levels in food samples through absorption of light, offering high sensitivity and precision. In contrast, CAF employs fluorescence techniques to measure mercury concentration, providing rapid results with minimal sample preparation. Both methods ensure accurate monitoring of mercury contamination in food items, essential for consumer safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
