<p><p>The Copper Foil Shielding Tape market is categorized into two main types: single sided and double sided. Single sided tape has conductive copper on one side, primarily used for grounding, shielding, and EMI/RFI interference reduction. Double sided tape features conductive surfaces on both sides, providing enhanced adherence and shielding capabilities, making it suitable for more complex applications requiring dual contact points. Both types are essential in electronics, automotive, and telecommunications for effective shielding and signal integrity.</p></p>
