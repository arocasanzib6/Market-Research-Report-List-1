<p><strong>The Covid-19 Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 testing market includes various types of diagnostic tools. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results, often within minutes, using immunological methods to detect antibodies or antigens. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a more detailed laboratory method that quantifies antibodies in the blood, offering greater sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, indicating immune response strength. Each method serves unique roles in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infection and immunity levels.</p></p>
