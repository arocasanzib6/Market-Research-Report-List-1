Supported metal catalysts, often utilized in hydrogenation and oxidation processes, leverage metals like platinum or palladium on carriers to boost activity. Zeolite catalysts, known for their porous structure, are widely used in petrochemical refining and environmental applications due to their selectivity and ability to facilitate complex reactions.</p></p>
