<p><p>The road freight transportation market is primarily divided into two types: Full Truckload (FTL) and Less-Than-Truckload (LTL). FTL involves transporting goods that fill an entire truck, typically resulting in faster delivery and reduced handling. In contrast, LTL combines shipments from multiple customers, making it cost-effective for transporting smaller loads. While FTL is ideal for large shipments requiring dedicated space, LTL caters to businesses with varying freight volumes, providing flexibility and minimizing shipping costs.</p></p>
