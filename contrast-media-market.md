<p><ul><li>X-ray & CT Contrast Media</li><li>MRI Contrast Media</li><li>Ultrasound Contrast Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Contrast Media market is segmented into three main types: X-ray & CT Contrast Media, MRI Contrast Media, and Ultrasound Contrast Media. X-ray & CT Contrast Media enhances image clarity for diagnostic purposes, utilizing iodine-based agents. MRI Contrast Media, primarily gadolinium-based, improves soft tissue visualization. Ultrasound Contrast Media, often microbubble agents, enhances echogenicity for better vascular imaging. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnostics, aiding in the identification and management of various medical conditions across diverse imaging modalities.</p></p>
