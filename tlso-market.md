<p><p>The TLSO (Therapeutic Lumbar Sacral Orthosis) market is divided into two primary types: soft braces and rigid braces. Soft braces provide moderate support and flexibility, often using elastic materials to allow movement while stabilizing the lower back. They are typically used for mild injuries or post-operative recovery. Rigid braces, on the other hand, offer maximum support and immobilization, employing hard materials to limit motion and provide stability for more severe conditions, spinal deformities, or extensive rehabilitation needs.</p></p>
