<p><p>The semiconductor dry pumps market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Cam Type**: Utilizes rotating cams to manage gas flow, ensuring efficient vacuum creation.</p><p>2. **Claw Type**: Features two interleaving claws that compress gases, offering reliable performance and energy efficiency.</p><p>3. **Combined Type (Roots + Claw)**: Integrates Roots and claw mechanisms for enhanced vacuum levels and flow rates, ideal for demanding applications.</p><p>4. **Screw Type**: Uses helical screws to compress gases, providing a continuous flow and stable performance for semiconductor processes.</p></p>
