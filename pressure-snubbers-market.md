<p><ul><li>Piston-Type Snubbers</li><li>Sintered Snubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure snubbers are devices used to dampen pressure surges in fluid systems, enhancing system reliability. The market is primarily categorized into two types: piston-type snubbers and sintered snubbers. Piston-type snubbers utilize a piston mechanism to absorb pressure spikes, providing quick response and effectiveness in high-pressure systems. In contrast, sintered snubbers are made from porous materials that allow controlled flow resistance, effectively reducing pressure fluctuations without moving parts, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal maintenance and durability.</p></p>
