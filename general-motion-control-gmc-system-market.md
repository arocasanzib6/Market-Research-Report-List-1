<p><p>The General Motion Control (GMC) system market is categorized into three main types: PLC-based, PC-based, and stand-alone systems. </p><p>PLC-based systems leverage programmable logic controllers for real-time motion control, offering flexibility and integration with industrial automation. PC-based systems utilize personal computers for advanced control applications, providing high processing power and complex algorithms. Stand-alone systems are self-contained units designed for specific tasks, making them ideal for simpler applications where integrated solutions are not required, ensuring ease of use and installation.</p></p>
