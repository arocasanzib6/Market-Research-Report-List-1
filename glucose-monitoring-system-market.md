<p><strong>The Glucose Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensors</li><li>Transmitters & Receivers</li><li>Integrated Insulin Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucose monitoring system market comprises three main types: sensors, transmitters and receivers, and integrated insulin pumps. Sensors are devices that detect glucose levels in interstitial fluid, providing real-time data. Transmitters and receivers facilitate the wireless transmission of glucose readings from sensors to monitoring devices, enhancing user convenience. Integrated insulin pumps combine glucose monitoring with insulin delivery, allowing for automated adjustments based on glucose levels. Together, these technologies improve diabetes management and support patient health outcomes.</p></p>
