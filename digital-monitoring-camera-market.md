<p><ul><li>Infrared digital surveillance camera</li><li>Non-infrared digital surveillance camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital monitoring camera market is segmented into two main types: infrared digital surveillance cameras and non-infrared digital surveillance cameras. Infrared cameras utilize infrared technology to capture clear images in low-light or complete darkness, making them ideal for nighttime surveillance. In contrast, non-infrared cameras rely on visible light for recording, offering high-resolution footage during daylight but limited performance in low-light conditions. Both types serve distinct security needs across residential, commercial, and industrial applications, enhancing overall safety and surveillance effectiveness.</p></p>
