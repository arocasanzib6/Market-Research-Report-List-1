<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System(EGCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Cleaning Systems (EGCS) are technologies designed to reduce emissions from ships, particularly sulfur oxides (SOx). The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop EGCS**: Utilizes seawater to scrub exhaust gases, discharging cleaned water back into the ocean.</p><p>2. **Closed Loop EGCS**: Employs a recirculating water system, treating and reusing water, ensuring no discharge into the sea.</p><p>3. **Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems**: Combines both methods, adapting to different operational conditions for efficient emission control.</p></p>
