<p><p>The surveillance (CCTV) lens market encompasses three primary types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses offer a constant focal length and are ideal for specific monitoring tasks where the field of view doesn't change. Varifocal lenses allow users to adjust the focal length within a range, providing flexibility in viewing angles without needing a complete lens change. Zoom lenses offer adjustable focal lengths with motorized capabilities, enabling dynamic tracking and varying focal distances, enhancing surveillance adaptability.</p></p>
