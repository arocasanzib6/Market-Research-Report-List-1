<p><p>LiDAR technology encompasses various market types, each tailored for specific applications. Aerial LiDAR uses aircraft to capture large-scale data over vast areas and is ideal for topographic mapping. Ground-based LiDAR involves stationary equipment for precise scanning of smaller regions, commonly used in surveying and civil engineering. Mobile LiDAR combines vehicle-mounted units to collect data while driving, useful for transportation and urban planning. UAV LiDAR employs drones for flexible, high-resolution surveying in hard-to-reach areas, enhancing data collection efficiency.</p></p>
