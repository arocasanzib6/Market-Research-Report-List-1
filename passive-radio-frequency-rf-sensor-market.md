<p><p>The passive radio-frequency (RF) sensor market is categorized into low, mid, and high frequency segments based on operating frequencies. Low frequency RF sensors, typically in the range of a few kilohertz to several megahertz, are suited for applications like RFID and basic communications. Mid frequency RF sensors, ranging from several megahertz to a few gigahertz, are often used in industrial and commercial monitoring. High frequency RF sensors operate above several gigahertz, enabling advanced applications like radar and high-resolution imaging.</p></p>
