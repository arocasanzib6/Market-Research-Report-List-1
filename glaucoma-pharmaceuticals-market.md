<p><strong>The Glaucoma Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prostaglandins Analogues (PGAs)</li><li>Non PGAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma pharmaceuticals market primarily consists of two types: Prostaglandin Analogues (PGAs) and Non-PGAs. PGAs are the most commonly prescribed medications, effective in increasing aqueous humor outflow to reduce intraocular pressure. Common examples include latanoprost and bimatoprost. Non-PGAs encompass a diverse range of medications, such as beta-blockers, carbonic anhydrase inhibitors, and alpha agonists, which work through various mechanisms to lower intraocular pressure. Both categories aim to manage glaucoma effectively and prevent vision loss. </p></p>
