<p><ul><li>Single Bladder Tourniquet</li><li>Dual Bladder Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic tourniquet market comprises primarily two types: single bladder and dual bladder tourniquets. Single bladder tourniquets feature one inflatable compartment, providing localized compression, suitable for various surgical procedures. In contrast, dual bladder tourniquets consist of two separate bladders, allowing for more controlled pressure distribution and enhanced blood flow management. This differentiation caters to specific surgical needs, improving patient outcomes and procedural efficiency. Both types are crucial in minimizing blood loss during operations and ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
