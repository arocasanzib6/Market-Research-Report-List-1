<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Medical Chillers</li><li>Water-Cooled Medical Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical chillers market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled medical chillers. Air-cooled medical chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them ideal for smaller facilities with limited water resources. They are easier to install and require less maintenance. In contrast, water-cooled medical chillers circulate water to absorb heat, typically offering higher efficiency and cooling capacity, making them suitable for larger medical facilities where consistent cooling is essential for equipment and processes.</p></p>
