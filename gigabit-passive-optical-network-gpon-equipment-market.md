<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) equipment market primarily consists of three key components: Optical Line Terminals (OLTs), Optical Network Terminals (ONTs), and Passive Optical Splitters. OLTs are central devices that connect to the service provider's backbone, managing data traffic and distributing signals to multiple users. ONTs are located at the customer premises, converting optical signals to electrical signals for end-user devices. Passive Optical Splitters distribute the optical signal from the OLT to multiple ONTs, enabling efficient network access without powered devices.</p></p>
