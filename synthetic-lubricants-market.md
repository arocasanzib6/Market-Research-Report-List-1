<p><p>The synthetic lubricants market comprises several key types. Poly-alpha-olefin (PAO) offers excellent thermal stability and low-temperature performance, making it ideal for automotive applications. Poly-alkylene-glycol (PAG) is water-soluble, providing superior lubricity and environmentally friendly options. Di-basic acid esters (Di-Ester) deliver high thermal and oxidative stability, suitable for high-performance applications. Polyol esters are known for their biodegradability and durability. Silicone lubricants offer unique properties like high-temperature resistance. Other types include esters and greases for specialized applications, broadening the market scope.</p></p>
